Overview

Purpose

The website will simulate the official website of a Brazilian internet company, which is trying to gain space in the international market, the website will contain the prices of internet plans, with beautiful colors and rounded buttons and cards, to attract the attention of customers, and facilitate the user experience, and will also have an about us page, where the customer can see more technical information about the company, to show that it is a reliable company.

Audience

Target audience:

  • Families looking for internet plans
  • Individuals looking for internet plans
  • Upper and middle class
  • People interested in technology
  • Home office workers
  • Gamers
  • Establishments with WiFi for customers
  • They seek a quality internet, and without bureaucracy
  • A good price
  • A trusted website
  • Many are frustrated with poor service
  • Most accesses are from desktop
